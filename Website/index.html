Many people, including potential clients of law firms who are researching their case or matter before hiring an attorney, use Google to find advice and answers to their questions. These prospective clients are also consuming content. They're currently looking for website content that will help them learn more about the problem they're dealing with and what they should do next. On our website, we use “Neobot”: A simple chatbot that will answer questions regarding the rights of the persons with disabilities act, the black money and imposition of tax act, the sexual harassment of women at the workplace act, and the prohibition of child marriage act. Consumers don't just read website content; they use it to make important decisions, such as contacting a law firm when they need legal help.

Learn About Neobot

NeoBot is a chat bot which uses artificial intelligence and machine learning to answer queries related to legal issues based on legal, tax and compliance using the knowledge it has gained from central acts. It is a user-friendly bot that will communicate with the user and will try to answer the questions related to the law in brief and if the user wishes to know more about the law it will give the option to download the document. The bot is advanced enough to convert the text to speech or the speech to text according to the user’s convenience. Thank you.
